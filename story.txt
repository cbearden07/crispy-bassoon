Jace stood at the edge of the balcony, the lights of the city lighting up the area around him as if it were daytime. He rested his hands on the railing in front of him and leaned forward casually, taking some of the weight off of his feet. The night was like any other, because here, nothing ever changed.

"I am free as a bird." Jace thinks to himself,free falling. At the very last moment of his life, he is finally starting to truly enjoy what he is doing.
